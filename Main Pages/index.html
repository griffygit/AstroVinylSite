Who We Are

Welcome to AstroVinyl, your haven for music enthusiasts and collectors alike!
Nestled in the heart of Beacon, our brick-and-mortar location boasts an extensive collection of physical media, spanning genres and generations. Step into our cozy space and immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of music history. From vintage classics to modern gems, our curated selection promises something for every discerning ear. Whether you're on the hunt for a rare vinyl gem or simply seeking to expand your musical horizons, AstroVinyl is your destination.

But AstroVinyl is more than just a record store; it's a vibrant community hub where music lovers converge. Join us during lounge hours to connect with like-minded individuals, share your latest finds, or simply soak in the eclectic ambiance. Experience the thrill of discovery as you browse our shelves, where each album tells a story waiting to be uncovered.

Our knowledgeable staff is always on hand to offer recommendations or engage in spirited discussions about your favorite artists and albums. So why wait? Come on down to AstroVinyl and let the music guide your journey. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a casual listener, there's always something special waiting for you at our Beacon location.
